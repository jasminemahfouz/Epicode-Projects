Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN :
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce



Ingredients


For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose

  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast

  • 11/2 teaspoons salt

  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced

  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top

  • neeses brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind oft witha paring knife if desied, though its not neccesary)

  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method


  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mixed together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don’t need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour…
